The Virtual Closet

Understanding and organizing your personal wardrobe can be difficult. The Virtual Closet is here to help!

Overview

The Virtual Closet is a proof-of-concept mobile application designed for COMP 590 - Design and Implementation of User Interfaces at UNC-CH.

The class guided students through the UX design process from conception to implementation. In addition to building prototypes and user flows, students also created user personas, conceptual models and conducted multiple user interviews.
